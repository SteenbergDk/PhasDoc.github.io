Oni

A very active and aggressive ghost. It manifests physically more often than other ghosts.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Freezing Temperatures
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Strength

  • More frequent ghost events
  • More visible manifestations

Weakness

Cannot perform “air ball” ghost events.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Normal line-of-sight acceleration
  • No special speed ability

How to Identify

  • Very frequent ghost events
  • Rarely (or never) does air ball events
  • Often appears physically
  • High activity overall

No Evidence

  • Wait for Air ball Ghost event
