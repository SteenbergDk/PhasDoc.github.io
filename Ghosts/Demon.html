Demon

One of the most aggressive ghosts. It can hunt more often than any other ghost.

Evidence

  • Freezing Temperatures
  • Ghost Writing
  • Ultraviolet

Strength

  • Can hunt at any sanity
  • Very short hunt cooldown

Weakness

Crucifix has increased range (5m instead of 3m).

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Speeds up with line of sight
  • No special speed ability

How to Identify

  • Very early hunts (even high sanity)
  • Frequent hunts close together
  • Crucifix works at longer range

No Evidence

  • Crucifix range is 5 meter.
  • Hunts more frequently
