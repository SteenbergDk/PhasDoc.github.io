Mare

A ghost that thrives in the dark. It becomes more aggressive when the lights are off.

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Orbs
  • Ghost Writing

Strength

  • More likely to hunt in the dark
  • Higher chance to turn lights off

Weakness

Less likely to hunt when lights are on.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Normal line-of-sight acceleration
  • No special speed ability

How to Identify

  • Turns lights off frequently
  • Hunts more in darkness
  • Rarely hunts in lit rooms
  • Turn on lights, instant turn off = mare
