Deogen

A ghost that always knows where you are. It cannot be hidden from, but it can be outrun.

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Writing
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Strength

  • Always knows player location
  • Cannot be hidden from (no hiding works)

Weakness

Moves extremely slow when close to the player.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Very fast when far away (~3 m/s)
  • Very slow when close (~0.4 m/s)
  • Always tracks player (line of sight irrelevant)

How to Identify

  • Ghost always finds you instantly
  • Cannot hide → it goes straight to you
  • Becomes very slow when near you
  • Unique heavy breathing in Spirit Box (close range)

No Evidence

  • Speed test the ghost from far distance and close distance - listen to the speed.
