Jinn

A territorial ghost that becomes faster when its victim is far away. It relies heavily on the power of electricity.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Ultraviolet
  • Freezing Temperatures

Strength

  • Moves faster when far from player
  • Can use ability to drain sanity

Weakness

Cannot use its ability if the breaker is off.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Fast when far away (~2.5 m/s)
  • Normal speed when close (1.7 m/s)
  • Only works when breaker is ON

How to Identify

  • Very fast at long distances
  • Slows down when close to player
  • Speed boost disappears if breaker is off
  • EMF spike at breaker (ability use)
