Goryo

A ghost that prefers to stay near its room. It is rarely seen without using special equipment.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Ultraviolet
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Strength

  • DOTS only visible through camera
  • Rarely leaves its favourite room

Weakness

Easier to track due to limited roaming.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Normal line of sight acceleration
  • No special speed ability

How to Identify

  • DOTS only visible on camera (not naked eye)
  • Stays very close to ghost room
  • Rare roaming behaviour
  • Rarely makes any events away from ghost room
