Spirit

The most common Spirit type in Phasmophobia. It has no special abilities, making it hard to identify.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box
  • Spirit Writing

Strength

No special strengths.

Weakness

Smudge Sticks prevent hunting for 180 seconds instead of the normal 90 seconds.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Speeds up with line of sight
  • Standard Spirit behaviour

How to Identify

  • Use smudge stick and time hunt delay
  • If ~3 min → likely Spirit
  • No unique behaviour → rule others out

No Evidence

  • Smudge and set countdown for 180 seconds. If the ghost hunts before that, it is likely a spirit.
