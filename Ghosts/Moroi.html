Moroi

A cursed ghost that feeds on sanity. The weaker its victims, the faster it becomes.

Evidence

  • Spirit Box
  • Ghost Writing
  • Freezing Temperatures

Strength

  • Moves faster at lower sanity
  • Can curse players through Spirit Box / Parabolic Mic

Weakness

Smudge Sticks blind the ghost for longer during hunts.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Speed increases as sanity drops
  • Very fast at low sanity (~2.5 m/s)
  • Normal LOS acceleration

How to Identify

  • Speed increases over time (as sanity drops)
  • Becomes extremely fast at low sanity
  • Sanity drains faster after Spirit Box interaction
  • Smudge blinds it longer than normal
  • -
  • Take sanity pills while hunting, to see if the speed decreases
