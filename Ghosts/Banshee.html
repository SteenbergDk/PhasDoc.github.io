Banshee

A singing ghost that targets one player at a time. Known for its unique hunting behaviour.

Evidence

  • Ultraviolet
  • Ghost Orbs
  • D.O.T.S Projector

Strength

  • Targets a single player during hunts
  • Can ignore other players completely

Weakness

Easier to identify due to unique targeting behaviour.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Speeds up with line of sight
  • Standard speed ghost

How to Identify

  • Only hunts one specific player
  • Other players may be ignored
  • Parabolic mic can detect unique scream

No Evidence

  • If the Banshee only target one player, or make a banshee scream, you can select this ghost type
