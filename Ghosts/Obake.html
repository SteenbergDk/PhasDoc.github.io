Obake

A shapeshifting ghost capable of manipulating its physical form. It leaves behind unique evidence when interacting with the environment.

Evidence

  • Ultraviolet
  • EMF Level 5
  • Ghost Orbs

Strength

  • Can leave unique fingerprints (6 fingers)
  • Fingerprints fade faster than normal

Weakness

  • Unique fingerprints reveal its identity.
  • Changes ghost type while hunting

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Normal line-of-sight acceleration
  • Can briefly shapeshift during hunts

How to Identify

  • 6-finger fingerprints (unique)
  • Fingerprints disappear faster
  • May not leave fingerprints every time
  • Can briefly change ghost model during hunts
