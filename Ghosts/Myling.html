Myling

A very vocal ghost known for its paranormal sounds. However, it is quieter than most ghosts during hunts.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Ultraviolet
  • Ghost Writing

Strength

  • Produces more paranormal sounds
  • More active on Parabolic Microphone

Weakness

Footsteps are quieter during hunts, making it harder to track.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Base speed: 1.7 m/s
  • Normal line-of-sight acceleration
  • No special speed ability

How to Identify

  • Footsteps are quieter than normal
  • Sounds stop at shorter distance (~12m)
  • More frequent paranormal sounds
  • Strong activity on Parabolic Mic
