Hantu

A ghost that thrives in the cold. The lower the temperature, the faster it moves.

Evidence

  • Freezing Temperatures
  • Ultraviolet
  • Ghost Orbs

Strength

  • Moves faster in colder areas
  • Very fast when breaker is off

Weakness

Moves slower in warm areas.

Speed & Line of Sight

  • Speed depends on temperature
  • Fast in cold, slow in warm
  • No standard line-of-sight speed boost

How to Identify

  • Speed changes between rooms
  • Faster when breaker is off
  • Visible freezing breath during hunts
